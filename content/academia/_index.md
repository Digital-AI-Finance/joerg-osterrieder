## COST Action CA19130: Fintech and AI in Finance
**Action Chair** | 2020 - Present

The COST Action brings together researchers from 38 European countries plus international partners to advance research in AI applications for finance.

### Objectives
1. Towards a European financial data space
2. AI for financial markets
3. Explainable and fair AI-generated decisions
4. Blockchain applications in finance
5. Sustainability of Digital Finance

### Network
- 200+ researchers across Europe
- 38 participating countries
- Academic institutions, regulators, industry partners
