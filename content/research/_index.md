**Role**: Action Chair

European research network focusing on:
- Towards a European financial data space
- AI for financial markets
- Explainable and fair AI-generated decisions
- Blockchain applications in finance
- Sustainability of digital finance

**Network**: 200+ researchers, 38 European countries, 5 international partners
