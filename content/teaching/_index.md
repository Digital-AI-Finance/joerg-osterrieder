## Teaching Philosophy

I believe in bridging theory and practice, combining rigorous academic foundations with real-world applications. My courses emphasize:

1. **Hands-on programming** - Python, machine learning libraries
2. **Real data** - Working with actual financial datasets
3. **Industry relevance** - Guest lectures from practitioners
4. **Research integration** - Latest findings from academia
