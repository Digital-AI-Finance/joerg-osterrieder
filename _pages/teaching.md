# Teaching

## Current Courses

### University of Twente, Netherlands

#### Reinforcement Learning in Finance
*Master's Level | Spring 2022-2025*

Developed 40% of curriculum. Covers reinforcement learning theory and applications to financial markets, including trading strategies, portfolio optimization, and market making.

#### Information Systems for the Financial Services Industry
*Master's Level | Spring 2022-2025*

Course coordinator. Module on AI in Finance covering machine learning applications, digital transformation, and fintech innovation.

#### Artificial Intelligence for Business
*Master's Level | Spring 2022-2024*

Module: Deep Reinforcement Learning in Finance. Advanced applications of deep learning and reinforcement learning to business and financial problems.

### Bern Business School, Switzerland

#### Digital Finance
*Bachelor's and Master's Level | Fall 2022-2024*

Developed curriculum on Machine Learning and AI applications in finance. Covers digital transformation, blockchain, and financial technology.

#### AI and ML for Finance
*Bachelor's Level | Fall 2022-2024*

Introduction to machine learning algorithms using Python. Practical applications to financial data and prediction problems.
