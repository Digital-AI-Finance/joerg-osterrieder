# Research Projects

## Current Major Projects

### MSCA Industrial Doctoral Network on Digital Finance
**Horizon Europe | EUR 4,500,000 | 2024-2027**
*Principal Investigator and Coordinator*

A Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions Industrial Doctoral Network advancing research in digital finance through collaboration among more than 20 institutions and 100 participating researchers from academia and industry. Key partners include the European Central Bank, ING Group, Deutsche Borse, and universities across Europe.

### COST Action FinTech and AI in Finance (CA19130)
**Horizon Europe | EUR 860,000 | 2020-2024**
*Principal Investigator and Action Chair*

Europe's largest research network on FinTech and AI in Finance with 400 academic members from 51 countries. Coordinated international research collaborations, organised major conferences, and established strategic partnerships across academia, industry, and regulatory bodies.

### Narrative Digital Finance
**Swiss National Science Foundation | CHF 236,118 | 2023-2025**
*Principal Investigator*

Investigating the role of narratives, structural breaks, and bubbles in digital financial markets using AI and natural language processing techniques. Partnership with Deutsche Borse's Quantitative Research team focusing on high-frequency trading and market microstructure.

### Network-Based Credit Risk Models in P2P Lending Markets
**Swiss National Science Foundation | CHF 347,836 | 2022-2025**
*Principal Investigator*

Developing novel machine learning approaches for credit risk assessment using network topology and centrality measures in peer-to-peer lending platforms.
