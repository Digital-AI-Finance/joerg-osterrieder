## About

I am a Professor specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Quantitative Finance. My research focuses on applying AI and ML techniques to financial markets, risk management, and digital finance.

I currently hold dual appointments at the University of Twente (Netherlands) and Bern Business School (Switzerland), where I teach and supervise research in machine learning, reinforcement learning, and AI applications in finance.
