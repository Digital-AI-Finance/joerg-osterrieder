Kristina Šutienė - EU COST Fin-AI
Kristina Šutienė
Kristina Sutiene holds Ph.D. in Informatics and M.S. in Applied Mathematics from Kaunas University of Technology (KTU). She currently works as an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematical Modelling at KTU.
She is a member of research group „Mathematical Modelling of Stochastic, Economic and Medical Systems“ and the researcher at the Centre of Artificial Intelligence at KTU. Kristina has been actively contributing in launching the MOOC on Artificial Intelligence in Lithuanian language.
Her research interests generally cover risk modelling and measuring by employing data science and artificial intelligence in the field of finance, economics and insurance.
Relevant projects:
LMTLT “Pension fund regulation during times of uncertainty in the context of sustainable investments”, S-MIP-21-32, 2021-2024
COST Action “Fintech and Artificial Intelligence in Finance - Towards a transparent financial industry”, CA19130, Coordinator - Zurich University of Applied Sciences, 2020-2024
H2020 project “FINancial Supervision and TECHnological Compliance training programme – FIN-TECH“, ICT-35-2018, Coordinator – Pavia University, 2019-2020
Erasmus+ KA2 project “MathE - Improve Math Skills in Higher Education“, Coordinator - Instituto Politécnico de Braganç, 2018 – 2021
ESF project “Establishment of the Competence Center for Analysis, Simulation and Risk Management (i.MAMC)", 10.1.1-ESFA-V-912-01-0001, Coordinator – State Tax Inspectorate Under the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Lithuania, 2016 – 2019
COST Action “Mathematics for Industry Network”, TD1409, Coordinator - Weierstrass Institute for Applied Analysis and Stochastics, 2017-2019
