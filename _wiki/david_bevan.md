David Bevan
David Bevan is a member of the MC nominated by Malta. Dr Bevan is a management education and critical management  scholar, principally driven by an interest in secular pragmatism. Internationally experienced professor in business ethics, strategy, social accounting, action research, etc.; author and management consultant. WWF/One Planet Education Network Fellow. Senior Fellow at Wicklander Institute of Business and Professional Ethics. Past President (UK) of European Business Ethics Network. Knowledge of Environmental Social and Governance impact / risk metrics and management; Ethics Expert in Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI), (Horizon 2020, European Commission) GDPR/ethical compliance expert (2017/21). Alumnus of King's College London; Sheffield Hallam University, The Prince of Wales's Business & Sustainability Programme at the University of Cambridge and University of Staffordshire. Academic Director of Academy of Business in Society network 2008-2012. Previously, 25 years experience in international business management and administration: trained in information technology, ERP, management accounting and reporting; human resource management and development. Work experience in USA, UK, France, Belgium and China.
