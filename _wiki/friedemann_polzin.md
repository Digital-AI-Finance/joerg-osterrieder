Friedemann Polzin

He holds a PhD in Business Economics from EBS Business School and a MSc in Business Administration from the University of Jena. His research focuses on the financing of sustainable innovation and entrepreneurship, corresponding organizational, legal and institutional arrangements as well as the political environment required for a transition towards a green economy.
