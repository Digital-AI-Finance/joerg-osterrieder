Audrius Kabašinskas - EU COST Fin-AI
Audrius Kabašinskas
Associate Professor, Researcher at Kaunas University of Technology (Lithuania).
His research interests include financial mathematics, risk measurement and management, pension system modeling, fraud detection.
Science projects:
• FINancial Supervision and TECHnological Compliance training programme - FIN-TECH 2019-01-01 - 2021-06-30
• Micro-foundations of Family Business: Dynamic Managerial Capabilities and Innovation Performance (FamBusDMC) 2017-11-02 - 2020-11-30
• Establishment of Competence Center for Analysis, Modeling and Risk Management 2015-11-13 - 2019-06-26
• COST Mathematics for Industry Network (MI-NET) 2015-05-05 - 2019-05-04
