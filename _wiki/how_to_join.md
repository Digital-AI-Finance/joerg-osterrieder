How to join - EU COST Fin-AI
How to join
If you are interested in our Action, and would like to join and contribute, please get in touch, following the below steps.
More information on participating in COST Actions, can be found
here
.
Join the
Fintech and AI meet-up group
and attend one of our COST events, e.g. the weekly virtual coffee session.

General interest
[
edit
]
I am generally interested in being involved, but do not want to take on an official role.
You are very welcome to join. Please sign up to the meetup:
Fintech and AI meet-up group
.
Also, please add your information and background to our
COST FinAI member profiles
as well as our
contact list
.
For MC members of COST countries
[
edit
]
Fill in the
new joiner questionnaires and information
.
Please also kindly schedule a meeting with the
MC members
of your country.
Please also kindly get in touch with the Action Chair
Joerg Osterrieder
or the Action Vice Chair
Valerio Poti
as well as the
working group leaders
.
Simultaneously, please add yourself to the
COST FinAI member profiles
and update the information on the
COST FinAI country profiles
. Please also kindly add one slide about yourself
here
and update your
country slide
.
Finally, choose your working group (you need approval from the WG leaders)
WG1
WG2
WG3
Once you have discussed the Action content with the above mentioned team, it is up to your National Coordinator in your country to nominate you for roles. The rules for that might be different for each country.
Sign up for additional
Responsibilities
.
International Partner Countries and Near-Neighbour Countries
[
edit
]
Kindly fill in the
new joiner questionnaire
.
Kindly fill in this
request for new international participants
. It is about YOU joining, so please emphasize the benefits for you.
Please pay particular attention to the benefits for you and the COST Action. This will be sent to all >125 MC members and they have to vote on that.
Please also kindly get in touch with the Action Chair
Joerg Osterrieder
or the Action Vice Chair
Valerio Poti
as well as the
working group leaders
.
Please add yourself to the
COST FinAI member profiles
and update the information on the
COST FinAI country profiles
. Please also kindly add one slide about yourself
here
and update your
country slide
.
Finally, choose your working group (you need approval from the WG leaders)
WG1
WG2
WG3
Once that is done, you will get an official invitation to e-COST, and your proposal can be submitted to the COST Science officer who, after approval, will then submit it to the COST FinAI management committee for approval.

Valerio Poti
